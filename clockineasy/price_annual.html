Frequently Asked Questions

How much does Scheduling cost?

After your trial period ends, Scheduling costs an additional $1 per user per month in your TSheets account. (with annual subscription)

Can TSheets set up my account for me?

Yes! We can help to setup your account AND train your employees on TSheets. Your time is important and we are happy to save you some! Contact us at 888–836–2720 to speak with one of our onboarding specialists.

What is a user?

Anyone who uses TSheets to track or manage time, including the administrator (the person who sets up and manages the TSheets account on your end).

How does the 14-day free trial work?

Simply sign up for a FREE trial, after 14 days add your credit card, and your account is ready to go!

Can I pay monthly instead of annually?

Yes. TSheets monthly pricing is $20 base fee + $5 per user. Sign-up for an annual subscription to save 20%!

What if I have more than 100 users?

Congratulations on your successful business! Annual pricing for 100+ users is $80 base fee + $4 for each user but the FREE trial is still available to try for 14 days. If you have additional questions please contact us at 999-999-999

Is Scheduling available as a standalone product?

Currently, Scheduling cannot be purchased as a standalone product. Have other scheduling questions? See answers to our most common questions./p>

Can I see a demo?

Definitely. Sign up for a 14-day free trial and register for a webinar so you can become wise and powerful in the ways of TSheets.

Do I need a credit card to sign up for the trial?

NEVER! Simply enter your credit card when you're ready to activate after your 14-day free trial and you're all set.

Do I have to sign a contract?

No. Haven't you ever heard the phrase "If you love something let it go?" We would never force you to stay, but hope you would want to!

Do you offer custom integrations?

Your wish is our command. Contact us and let's integrate!

Is support included during my trial and for my activated account?

Absolutely! Regardless of how many employees you have, if you're still in your free trial, or if you've been a loyal customer for years, support is always free and unlimited. Call us, email us, chat with us at any time, we want to hear from you!
